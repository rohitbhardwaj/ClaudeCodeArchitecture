# Scoring Rubric — Enterprise Rollout Readiness Score

## 5 — Excellent

Participant scores based on evidence, recommends the right rollout level, and identifies prioritized improvements.

## 4 — Strong

Participant scores most areas well and recommends reasonable restrictions.

## 3 — Acceptable

Participant provides a readiness score but lacks strong evidence or restrictions.

## 2 — Weak

Participant bases recommendation on opinion more than evidence.

## 1 — Poor

Participant recommends broad rollout without controls.
