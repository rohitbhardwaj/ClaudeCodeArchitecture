# Scoring Rubric — Production Incident Role Play

## 5 — Excellent

Group identifies:

```text
behavior regression
coupon validation semantics
PII logging risk
test weakening
missing quality gates
customer impact
business impact
missing CLAUDE.md rules
governance failure
```

Produces a strong 5-part incident review.

## 4 — Strong

Group identifies most major issues but may miss one category such as support reason codes or CODEOWNER review.

## 3 — Acceptable

Group identifies some technical and customer issues but does not connect them clearly to governance.

## 2 — Weak

Group focuses mostly on debugging and does not identify missing prompt constraints or review gates.

## 1 — Poor

Group blames only the AI and does not identify architecture controls that would have prevented the incident.
