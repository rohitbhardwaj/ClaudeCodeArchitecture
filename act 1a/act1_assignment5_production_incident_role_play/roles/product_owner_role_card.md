# Role Card — Product Owner

## Your Perspective

You represent customer experience, business impact, revenue, and support readiness.

## Your Investigation Focus

Look for:

```text
customer checkout failures
valid coupon rejection
cart abandonment
support ticket increase
revenue impact
trust impact
launch impact
```

## Incident Symptoms

```text
checkout error rate increased
valid coupons rejected
support tickets increased
customers abandoned carts
support could not explain failures clearly
```

## What You Should Notice

```text
coupon behavior changed without product approval
support reason codes became less useful
customer trust was damaged
promotion campaign performance may be affected
conversion may drop
support costs may rise
```

## Your Role Task

Answer:

```text
1. What customer impact occurred?
2. What business metrics were affected?
3. What should product have approved?
4. What support readiness was missing?
5. What CLAUDE.md rule would protect business behavior?
```

## Recommended Product Rule

```text
Claude must not change customer-visible checkout, coupon, pricing, tax, refund, or error-message behavior without product approval and characterization tests.
```
