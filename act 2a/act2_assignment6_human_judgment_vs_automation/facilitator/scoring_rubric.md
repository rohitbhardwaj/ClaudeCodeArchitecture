# Scoring Rubric — Human Judgment vs Automation

## 5 — Excellent

Participant correctly classifies most responsibilities and explains accountability boundaries.

Mentions:

```text
human accountability
production risk
API contracts
payment behavior
release decisions
rollback
shared validation
```

## 4 — Strong

Participant classifies most responsibilities correctly and identifies at least one decision currently over-influenced by tools.

## 3 — Acceptable

Participant understands the categories but may over-automate shared responsibilities.

## 2 — Weak

Participant treats too many judgment decisions as automatable.

## 1 — Poor

Participant does not distinguish automation from accountability.
