# Scoring Rubric — Classification Exercise

## 5 — Excellent

Participant correctly distinguishes all three levels and explains why Level 2 and Level 3 tasks require stronger controls.

Strong answer includes:

```text
scope
blast radius
repo context
workflow impact
human approval
quality gates
```

## 4 — Strong

Participant classifies most use cases correctly and identifies at least one governance need.

## 3 — Acceptable

Participant understands the levels but may classify some repo-aware tasks as simple code-helper tasks.

## 2 — Weak

Participant treats most use cases as simple coding tasks and misses workflow risk.

## 1 — Poor

Participant does not distinguish local code generation from repo-aware or workflow-level activity.
