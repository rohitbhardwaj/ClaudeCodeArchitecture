# Scoring Rubric — Blast Radius Before Implementation Kata

## 5 — Excellent

Participant identifies business, technical, financial, security, operational, testing, rollback, and approval impacts.

## 4 — Strong

Participant identifies most major impacts but misses one area such as tax, refunds, or fraud.

## 3 — Acceptable

Participant identifies code/API/testing impacts but misses business/financial/operational impacts.

## 2 — Weak

Participant mainly thinks about implementation files.

## 1 — Poor

Participant jumps directly to coding without blast-radius analysis.
